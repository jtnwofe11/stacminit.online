STACMINIT FOUNDATION

INTRODUCTION:

STUDENT TALENT AND CAREER MENTORING INITIATIVE (STACMINIT)

STACMINIT is an acronym as captured in the heading above.
It was birthed in 2018 after a very long incubation period by Williams Obinna Nwofe (WON ).
It is a twin foundation program aimed at providing the students in the tertiary institutions in
Nigeria with a twin opportunity of mentorship on the various career progression of their choice and nurturing of their various God-given talents.
This is done with the aim to encourage talent hatching amongst these students and a robust
encouragement on their career pursuit in order to know which of them that will better put food on
their table at their point of graduation.

STACMINIT:

STACMINIT as an NGO is Africanized due to the peculiar problem it is trying to solve in the continent.
It is educational/ professional based foundation. Its aim is to curb the problem faced by a potential incoming student of the university and other
tertiary institutions over career choice making and bring to their knowledge,
the need to hacked to their talent which might even put better food on their table.

MOTIVATION:

Africa and Nigeria in particular show careless interest to talents, but adore paper qualification.
To this end, people hustle for paper qualifications by all means possibly abandoning their already perceived talents which they exhibit effortlessly.
Because of the quest to answer graduate, a lot of mushroom tertiary institutions have emerged
with half-baked graduates who can't compete favourably with their counterparts anywhere in the world.
Most of these graduates can only be useful to
Nigerian Labour Market
and not to the global market. These crop of graduates kill the economy of the country through corruption.
On the talent aspect, they would have successfully killed their talent where they left them to pursue academic careers.

OBJECTION:

To properly guide the students on the prerequisite technical know-how in career choice making that will activate
their full interest in their choices of career. To place careers and talents side by side for these students such that a better one can successfully feed them in future.
To stand as a gap between these students
and the institutions in providing orientation to the vulnerable students which may appear marooned and naive to the working
of the tertiary institution. To follow up on these students as they
progress in their career and talents to ensure adequate output. To start up career and talent guide from
the secondary school to properly tame the observed destinies of these students to their
supposed destinations. We provide legal and human right aids to
any mistreated student. This we do knowing that Nigerian tertiary institutions record
high degree of misconduct from lecturers and even fellow students. In all, we provide mentorship
role to not only the in-coming students, but to the undergraduates.
